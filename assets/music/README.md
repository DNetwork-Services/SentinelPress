# Background music

Drop royalty-free music tracks here (`.mp3`, `.wav`, or `.m4a`) and the reel voiceover step will randomly pick one and mix it under the narration at low volume, looping/trimming it to match each reel's length automatically.

**Why this folder is empty by default:** music licensing needs a human to actually read and accept the terms — that's not something to automate sight-unseen. A few good free sources where you can verify the license yourself:

- **YouTube Audio Library** (studio.youtube.com → Audio Library) — filter for "no attribution required"
- **Pixabay Music** (pixabay.com/music) — free for commercial use, no attribution required
- **Free Music Archive** (freemusicarchive.org) — check each track's specific CC license

If this folder is empty, reels still get a voiceover — they just won't have background music.
